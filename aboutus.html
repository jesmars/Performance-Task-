About Us

We are Students from Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Pinas City and this our Performance Task: The Global Citizen's Showcase in GE103

Our Objective

The objective of this research is to analyze the different forms of pollution, understand their causes and effects, and examine how they influence global systems such as the economy, society, and politics. This study also aims to explore the specific impacts of pollution in the Philippine context, highlighting real statistics and local case studies to show the urgency of the issue. By presenting these insights, the research seeks to raise awareness, encourage responsible environmental behavior, and promote solutions that support a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future for all.
